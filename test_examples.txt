# Example News for Testing Fake News Detection Model
# These are example political statements for testing the 5-class classifier

## =====================================================
## FALSE / PANTS ON FIRE EXAMPLES (Misinformation)
## =====================================================

FAKE_1: "The government is secretly adding microchips to vaccines to track citizens' movements and control their behavior through 5G networks."

FAKE_2: "Scientists discovered that the Earth is actually flat and NASA has been hiding the truth for decades."

FAKE_3: "Drinking bleach or injecting disinfectant can cure COVID-19 according to medical experts."

FAKE_4: "The 2020 election was stolen through millions of illegal votes from dead people and foreign interference."

FAKE_5: "Climate change is a hoax invented by China to hurt American manufacturing."


## =====================================================
## TRUE / MOSTLY TRUE EXAMPLES (Verified Facts)
## =====================================================

TRUE_1: "NASA successfully launched the Artemis I mission, an uncrewed flight around the Moon, in November 2022."

TRUE_2: "The unemployment rate in the United States fell to 3.4% in January 2023, the lowest level since 1969."

TRUE_3: "President Biden signed the Inflation Reduction Act in August 2022, which includes provisions for climate change and healthcare."

TRUE_4: "The World Health Organization declared COVID-19 a global pandemic on March 11, 2020."

TRUE_5: "India became the fourth country to successfully land a spacecraft on the Moon with Chandrayaan-3 in August 2023."


## =====================================================
## HALF TRUE EXAMPLES (Partially Accurate)
## =====================================================

HALF_1: "The economy is the strongest it has ever been in American history."

HALF_2: "Illegal immigration is at an all-time high at the southern border."

HALF_3: "Tax cuts always pay for themselves through economic growth."

HALF_4: "Social Security will run out of money in the next decade."

HALF_5: "Electric vehicles are completely emissions-free and better for the environment."
